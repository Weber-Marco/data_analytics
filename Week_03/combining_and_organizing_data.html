Compare between area and price_per_m2¶

There is a negative Relationship between the average area and average price per square meter.

As the area increases the price per square meter seems to decrease.

It is explained there is a hidden base cost for every apartment and/or additional cost that doesnt translate to bigger apartments (kitchen, bathrooms)
